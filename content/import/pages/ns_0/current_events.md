### Conferences



Forthcoming conferences of interest to PAWS Lab members can be found
on [WikiCFP](http://www.wikicfp.com/), user PAWS:
<http://www.wikicfp.com/cfp/servlet/event.showlist?lownerid=19251&ltype=w>
. To add a missing conference, login as user *paws* and add it to your
list.

###### Research Talks

Forthcoming research talks at Pitt
and CMU of interest to PAWS Lab members can be found on [CoMeT
system](http://halley.exp.sis.pitt.edu/comet/), group Personalized
Adaptived Web Systems Group (PAWS):
<http://halley.exp.sis.pitt.edu/comet/community.do?comm_id=1> . To add a
missing talk, simply add it to PAWS group.
