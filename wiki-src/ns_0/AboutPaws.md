## PAWS Lab

\n\nPersonalized Adaptive Web Systems Lab was established in 2004 with
support from National Science Foundation and School of Information
Sciences. The goal of the PAWS Lab is development and evaluation of
innovative user- and group-adaptive Web-based technologies, systems, and
architectures. The Lab currently explores a range of user modeling,
adaptation and personalization technologies. The work of the Lab is
supported by NSF and DARPA funding.\n\n== Try PAWS Tools at our
Community Portal == \n\nGo to [Knowledge
Tree](http://adapt2.sis.pitt.edu/kt) portal and [create trial
account](http://adapt2.sis.pitt.edu/kt/register.html) to try our
tools.\n\nIf you are a researcher and would like to quickly try our
adaptation tools, create the trial account (link above) and proceed
