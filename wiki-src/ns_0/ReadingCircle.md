## Overview

\nReadingCircle is a <a href="social_navigation" class="wikilink"
title="social navigation">social navigation</a> and
<a href="social_comparison" class="wikilink"
title="social comparison">social comparison</a> interface to engage
students into online reading and facilitate the reading and navigation
process \n\n== Project ==\n\n\* <a
href="Engaging_Students_in_Online_Reading_Through_Social_Progress_Visualization"
class="wikilink"
title="Engaging Students in Online Reading Through Social Progress Visualization">Engaging
Students in Online Reading Through Social Progress Visualization</a>\n\n==
