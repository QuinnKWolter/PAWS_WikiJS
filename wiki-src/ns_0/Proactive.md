## Link: [<http://proactive.sis.pitt.edu>](http://proactive.sis.pitt.edu)

\n\nProactive is an adaptive job recommender system which helps
information technology-related job seekers find relevant opening jobs in
the United States. The recommendation in this system is based on
content. In order to take advantage of contents related to job cases,
various semantic structures are being used. The system also provides
comprehensive ways to explore job information, from simple navigable
list of information to highly personalized recommendation.
